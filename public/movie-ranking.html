Akingbade's MOVIE RANKING PROJECT

My Top 3 Movies

1. BROTHERHOOD - The Movie

Story of Orphaned twin brothers living opposite lives. Shaped up in the exact same streets of lagos, but shaped into different paths of life.
Wale Adetula is a Police Officer and his twin brother, Akin Adetula a hardened criminal
Featuring: Folarin "Falz" Falana, Tobi Bakare and Ronke Oshodi-Oke

2. QUAM'S MONEY

Quam's Money follows the adventures of security guard-turned-millionaire 'Quam' played by Folarin 'Falz' Falana as he navigates the world with his newfound wealth.
Featuring: Falz, Nse Ikpe-Etim, Uchemba Williams

3. JAGUN JAGUN

A young man determined to become a mighty warrior joins an elite army, encountering the wrath of a maniacal warlord and the love of a woman.
Featuring: Lateef Adedimeji, Femi Adebayo, Fathia Balogun
